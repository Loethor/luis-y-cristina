How to get there

Information about arriving by car, train, bus, and parking options.

Address

The wedding venue is De Floriana, located at the entrance of Molinaseca. You will find it on the right-hand side coming from the Ponferrada road. Floriana has no parking, but there is free parking nearby.

View location on Google Maps

Arriving by car

Molinaseca is 7 km from Ponferrada. You can easily reach it via the LE-142 road. Floriana has no parking, but there is free parking nearby.

Arriving by plane

  • Oviedo (Asturias): 2h 30min by car
  • Santiago de Compostela: 2h 30min by car
  • Madrid: 4h by car

There is public transportation to Ponferrada, but we recommend renting a car.
